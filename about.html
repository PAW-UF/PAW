About Us

People for Animal Wellness (PAW) was founded in Spring 2010. We focus on fundraising and volunteering for local rescues as well as learning about the domestic and native animals of Florida. We hold biweekly meetings where we hear from various speakers, and we plan exciting field trips that provide our members with a new outlook about the animals that they encounter. We volunteer with a wide range of organizations, including those that protect dogs, cats, tigers, horses, pigs, cows, monkeys, bats, and many other animals.
