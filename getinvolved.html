Events and GBMs

PAW hosts biweekly GBMs in CSE A101 on Mondays, 7-8pm. These are great opportunities to learn about local animal rescues and conservation efforts and to meet likeminded people.

There are no dues or requirements to come to a GBM; just show up! To attend a volunteering event or social, all we ask is that you add your name to the signup if one is hyperlinked. If you need to cancel, do so at least 48 hours before the start of the event.
